What We Collect

We collect your email address and password when you create an account. We also store the stories you choose to publish to the Story Wall, and your video creation history.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the YES! service — to create your account, generate your videos, and display your stories if you choose to share them. We never sell your data to third parties.

Your Stories

Stories you submit remain yours. You own your content. YES! is granted a licence to display stories you choose to publish publicly on the Story Wall. You can request deletion of your content at any time.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only — to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Your API keys (if applicable) are encrypted at rest.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To make a request, contact us at hello@yes-platform.com.
