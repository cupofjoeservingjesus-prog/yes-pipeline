Questions Answered

Everything you
need to know.

No surprises. No fine print. Just honest answers.

Is it really free to start? +

Yes — completely free. You get 3 full cinematic videos the moment you sign up. No credit card needed. No catch. We want you to experience YES! before you ever spend a dollar.

Do I need any video or tech skills? +

Zero. If you can type a few sentences on your phone, you can create a cinematic film with YES! Our AI handles everything — the script, the visuals, the music, the editing. You just tell your story.

Who owns my story and my video? +

You do. Always. Your story is yours. YES! never sells, shares or uses your content without your permission. You can delete your stories at any time.

Does it work on my phone? +

Yes — YES! works on any phone, tablet or computer. No app download needed. Just open your browser and go. Designed mobile-first so it feels beautiful on your screen.

Can I share my video to TikTok and Instagram? +

Absolutely — once your video is created you can share directly to TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, WhatsApp and more in one tap. The caption is written for you automatically.

What happens after my 3 free videos? +

After your 3 free videos you can choose a plan starting at just $9.99/month CAD. There's no pressure and no automatic charge. You decide when and if you want to upgrade.

Is my story private or does everyone see it? +

Your choice entirely. Your videos are private by default — only you can see them. You decide if and when to share to the Story Wall or to social media. Nothing is ever posted without your action.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes — cancel anytime, instantly, with no fees or penalties. We believe in earning your loyalty every single month — not trapping you in a contract.
